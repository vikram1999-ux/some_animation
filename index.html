Our Story

Hi there I’m Nikki, and as a Mum, we really only want what’s best for our little ones and our families, that we try so hard to provide the best we can for them, in hope that they live a long, healthy, happy and prosperous life. We want to protect them from harm and help build a strong foundation and provide them with the tools they will need to thrive in their lives.

…It was one day after work, I picked up my daughter from her Grandmother’s house, and as my daughter was not known for having midday naps often, her Grandmother was so thrilled to tell me that, that day she slept for 2 hours straight. Her Grandmother then said to me “don’t worry, I turned off the ‘Wefee’(Wi-Fi) machine in her room.” I looked at her confused and replied “why it’s ok, isn’t it?” And she replied” I don’t think so, isn’t it like radiation?” We both looked at each other, stumped, and realized how little we knew about the safety and effects of Wi-fi and wireless devices. As ubiquitous as Wi-fi is, the average user really doesn’t know much about it. So she got me thinking, and down the rabbit hole I went…and Eve Fleur was born.

How do Eve Fleur smart wear products work?

Our products have been designed by inserting a special lightweight conductive Silver blended fabric, that provides a high shielding efficacy of 44-46D, providing protection against non-inoizing EMF and RF radiation emitted from Wi-Fi, Mobile phones, Baby Monitors, Tablets and countless other wireless technologies.

For comfort, we’ve carefully selected a premium quality Bamboo Viscose blended jersey, that offers breathability, high stretchability, moisture wicking benefits, is easy to launder, and feels absolutely soft and luxurious to touch. When combined together, the wearer will have both EMF protection, and enjoy the ultrasoft handfeel of the Bamboo finish.
